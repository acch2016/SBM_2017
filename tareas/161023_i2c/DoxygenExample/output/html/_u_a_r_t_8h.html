uint8  character

It sends one character through the serial port. This function should include the next sentence: while(!(UART0_S1 & UART_S1_TC_MASK)). It is to guaranty that before to try to transmit a byte, the previous one was transmitted. In other word, to avoid to transmit data while the UART is busy transmitting information.
